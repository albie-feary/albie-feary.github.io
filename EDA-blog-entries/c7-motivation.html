Motivation

Looking through the pleasure, passion, purpose lense.

Mr Meng has great insights into finding motivation at work and that is to find your higher purpose. Most people spend their lives chasing pleasure (think rockstar, shortlived highs.) Beyond this they spend a little time pursuing their passion and achieving flow but very seldom spend time and energy working on their higher purpose. The key difference in these three types of happiness are their sustainability. Pleasure is shortlived whereas working towards a higher purpose is the most sustainable form of happiness. In order to find out what motivates us in our work it then makes sense to look for our higher purpose. Because when this happens, our work can be a susatainable source of happiness! And when we are happy doing that work, we get better at it, reaching great flow in our passion. And when you get good at your work, you then get recognition for that work which is the rockstar happiness that is shortlived but relished in small doses.
