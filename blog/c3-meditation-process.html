Process Over Product

In taking the first leap of deleting the HTML and CSS files from Sprint Two, this idea of process over product really helped. Its not the product that is of value, its the process!Now isn't that a hugely comforting thing?

So essentially, so long as I give this all my very best effort to understand and implement concepts, it does not matter what the actual product looks like.

The point is the learning.
