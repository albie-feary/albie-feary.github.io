Riding your emotions like a horse

When Programming, what are some of your triggers?

Triggers in programming are brought on by a number of things. When code doesn't run as expected, when a problem to be solved is beyond my grasp...
When programming I try to stay in a state of amusement and frustration, laughing at myself for what I dont know rather than berating myself for this exponential, never ending learning process.

A disproportionate response occurs, however, when I cannot solve a problem that has been around for the day/night/day. This is when self doubt, a feeling of being an imposter and questioning of many life choices come in! A drastic response indeed... My amusement state is replace by a few horrid monsters! I keep reminding myself that my emotions are not me and to begin my thoughts with kindness, humor and compassion and so starve out those lurking monsters!

The ultimate goal of the chapter and the idea of self regulation is to befriend ones emotions, all of them no matter whether theyre kind or malice, joy or sorrow.
Let them in and let them be.

How did you find the Siberian North Railroad exercise?

This analogy of a train stopped in its tracks heading north in snowy Siberia is great for remembering to Stop and Breathe. This then allows time to Notice and maybe Reflect and then Respond appropriately. The most important part is remember to Breathe, as everything flows from this point.
