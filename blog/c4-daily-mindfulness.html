Zen and a walking baby

An analogy that Meng writes about is that learning to reach a certain state of mind is like a baby leaerning to walk. This is great. So far I'm just a zen baby who is making her first steps into this realm. I've gained what he describes as “initial access” where I can acess a certain state of mind but cannot maintain it for very long. With time and practice comes “consolidation.” This is very the long process of taking those first wobbly steps to being able to walk around the house. The lesson learnt is to not be discouraged if your progression seems to be very slow.
